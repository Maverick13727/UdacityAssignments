Jaguar

Jaguars can see six times better than humans due to a layer of tissue in the back of the eye that reflects light.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera onca
  • Basic Habitat:Americas
  • Average Length: 4-6 feet
  • Average Lifespan 12-15 years

The jaguar is the largest cat in the Americas having a compact body, a broad head and powerful jaws. Its coat is normally yellow and tan, but the color can vary from reddish brown to black.
